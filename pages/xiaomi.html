Overview

Xiaomi has quickly become a formidable player in the smartphone industry, known for delivering high-quality devices at competitive prices. Since the launch of its first smartphone in 2011, Xiaomi has revolutionized the market with innovative features like MIUI software, high-resolution cameras, and powerful processors. As a technology leader, Xiaomi has consistently pushed the boundaries of what's possible in mobile technology. The Mi and Redmi series have redefined user expectations with features such as fast charging, AI-enhanced photography, and edge-to-edge displays. Xiaomi offers a diverse range of smartphones, from budget-friendly models to premium flagships, appealing to a global audience. Learn more on the official Xiaomi website.

About

Founders Xiaomi was founded on April 6, 2010, by Lei Jun and a group of partners, with the vision of making high-quality technology accessible to everyone.
Current Leadership Xiaomi is currently led by Lei Jun, who serves as the Chairman and CEO, guiding the company’s strategy and growth.
Industry Recognition Xiaomi is recognized for disrupting the smartphone industry with its innovative approach to technology and cost-efficiency, earning a strong reputation in markets worldwide.
Global Influence Xiaomi has rapidly expanded its global influence, becoming one of the top smartphone manufacturers globally, with a significant presence in Asia, Europe, and other markets.
Key Products Xiaomi's major product lines include Mi and Redmi smartphones, Mi TVs, smart home products, and wearable devices like the Mi Band.
Market Capitalization Xiaomi has seen impressive growth in market capitalization since its IPO in 2018, reflecting its strong market presence and investor confidence.
Retail Presence Xiaomi products are available in over 90 countries, with a growing number of Mi Stores and partnerships with online and offline retailers.
Brand Value Xiaomi has established itself as one of the most valuable tech brands globally, recognized for its innovation, quality, and affordability.
