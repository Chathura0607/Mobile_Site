  4. Summary

    The Xiaomi Mix Flip is a cutting-edge foldable smartphone that combines innovation with elegance. It features a 6.86-inch Foldable LTPO AMOLED main display and a 4-inch external AMOLED screen, both offering rich colors, Dolby Vision, and a 120Hz refresh rate for an unparalleled visual experience.

    Powered by the Qualcomm Snapdragon 8 Gen 3 chipset and equipped with a robust 4780 mAh battery, the Mix Flip ensures top-tier performance and long battery life. Its advanced dual-camera system with 50 MP wide and telephoto lenses allows for stunning photography and 8K video recording.

    The Mix Flip's sleek design includes a glass and aluminum build, and it offers 5G connectivity, making it both stylish and future-proof. With ample storage options and UFS 4.0 technology, it meets all your multitasking needs. The side-mounted fingerprint sensor adds an extra layer of security and convenience. Learn more.
