Summary

The Samsung Galaxy M35 is a sleek and powerful smartphone designed to deliver a high-quality user experience. It features a 6.6-inch Super AMOLED display with a 120Hz refresh rate, providing vibrant colors and smooth scrolling.

Equipped with the Exynos 1380 chipset and a substantial 6000 mAh battery, the Galaxy M35 ensures excellent performance and long-lasting battery life. The versatile camera setup, including a 50 MP main sensor, allows for impressive photo and video quality.

With a modern design and solid build quality, including a glass back and plastic frame, the Galaxy M35 is both stylish and durable. It supports 5G connectivity for fast data speeds and offers various memory options to suit different needs. The device also features a side-mounted fingerprint sensor for added security. Learn more.
